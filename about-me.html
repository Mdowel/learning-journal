Hi there! My name is Michelle and welcome to my learning journal.

I'm a newcomer to the vibrant world of web development. Having previously worked in healthcare, I've embarked on an exciting journey of career change, drawn to the dynamic and creative realm of frontend development.

Combining two worlds

With a background in healthcare, I bring a unique perspective to my newfound passion for coding. While the two fields may seem worlds apart, I've discovered surprising parallels between working with medical conditions and troubleshooting code. Both require keen attention to detail, problem-solving skills, and a commitment to continuous learning.

Just getting started

Right now, I'm diving into a self-guided course for frontend development, eager to absorb as much knowledge and experience as I can. What intrigues me most about web development is the sense of puzzle-solving it offers. Every line of code feels like finding a piece that fits just right, and when they all come together, it's so rewarding to see something not only work, but look great too. As someone who thrives on visual feedback, frontend development provides the perfect blend of creativity and technicality. I find great satisfaction in crafting user interfaces and bringing designs to life, knowing that every line of code contributes to a seamless and engaging web experience.

In this new chapter of my professional journey, I'm excited to combine my background in healthcare with my burgeoning skills in web development, striving to create meaningful and impactful solutions that improve people's lives in the digital sphere. I'm ready to make a positive mark in the ever-evolving world of technology!
